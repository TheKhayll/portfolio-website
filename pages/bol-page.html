Bringer of Light

Genre: Action-RPG, Adventure, Story-Driven

Game Engine: Unreal Engine 5

Date: November 11, 2023

An ongoing game that i am developing right now, The game is a Third-Person, Action-RPG Adventure and Story-Driven. The main story of it is discovering Ahon's existence and throughout his adventures he discovers the truth about his goal in the labyrinth world.

This game is being developed right know and there are major revisions being made before its full release. But this game is the game that i am being the most ambitious about that is why there are so many elements that is being implemented in this game. This is inspired by Genshin Impact and God of War in terms of few Graphical Designs and Gameplay.

Action Combat Game

Genre: Action

Game Engine: Unreal Engine 4

Date: April 15, 2024

I developed this game where i followed and completed an online course that primarily focused on the development of the game in pure C++ in Unreal Engine, it showcases the very fundamentals of how an Action Combat mechanics should be.

During the development of this project, i understood what are the capabilities of using C++ in Unreal Engine is and i understood that it can be quite powerful to mix C++ and Blueprint Scripting in Unreal Engine, commonly known as Hybrid Programming.

Lock-On, Box Tracing, Attack Combos, Boss Fights, State Machines, A.I Behaviors, Health and Stamina System, and Environment Building Fundamentals.

About Me

Hi, I'm John Angelo Mikhail A. My main focus is to create games that are entertaining to play, have immersive storyline, breathtaking environments and a meaningful and expressive music; with all of these combined i am able to create games that leaves the players closely in touch with my own crafts.

Who Am I?

Currently 21 years old, living with my parents in Caloocan City, Metro Manila, Philippines.
A 3rd Year Student in Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Quezon City Branch.
Currently taking my Bachelor's Degree for Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.
Primarily work with myself in terms of Game Development.

How my Game Development Career Started?

It started because of my Father, he exposed me to games when i was a child; games such as Plants vs. Zombies and Angry Birds, my imagination in creating my own fantasies of games spouted by making my own toys with paper.
My interest and skills grew when i reached my teenage years where i explored more about software development and discovered that i can develop my games through Game Engines like Unity, Unreal Engine, Godot, etc.
I started creating my own simple text based game by using Batch Programming Language, and later i decided i want to create 3D Games and went with Unreal Engine 4.

My Philosophy

My main philosophy in my Game Development career is that "Games are work of art, they are crafted through the hands and minds of those who dream to see and feel the world that comes in the depths of their feelings and imagination. A game is not just a game but it is a complex system of creativity and expression."

Tools I Use

I primarily use Unreal Engine 4.27 for my Game Development due to its stability and that many successful games are created by this version of Epic Games' Game Engine. I use Blender for my 3D Modelling and Animations. I use FL Studio for my Music Production and Sound Designs. I use Photoshop for creating my textures and assets which are tied with my 3D Modelling and most of the User Interfaces in the game. I use Audacity for any voice actings. I use Notion/Notion Calendar for tracking my tasks and setting deadlines for my projects.

I also do Music Composition in my free time trying to practice my skills in composing my own scores and i love observing games when i play so that i can grab ideas on how it was implemented or how it was structured.

My Skills

Programming Proficiency

I have been studying and applying the C language family for almost 5 years, i can say that i am proficient in the C family programming languages: C, C# and C++, which i use most of the time whenever i am scripting my games through my preferred Game Engines.

Music Composition/Production

I studied a little about Music Theory and i am applying this in my own games for setting the environment, atmosphere and the mood, i usually do this in my free time as a relaxation and as an outlet for my emotions.

Critical Observation

Whenever i am playing games, i'm not only playing them for entertainment but rather as a research and observation for me to think of a process on how the systems are implemented, how do they work behind the curtains, i always do this in every game and i always observe every elements that are present in it.

Gameplay Design

Level Designer

Storytelling and Narrative Designer

I enjoy creating stories out of my experiences which can then be translated into imaginations; i always want to make my stories immersive and as relatable as possible to allow it to touch the players' emotions and immersion.

3D Modelling and Texturing

Animation and Rigging

Persistence

Attentive to Details

When developing my games i often focus on the details of every elements i have in my game and take notes of it to make sure i must either fix, re-model and re-design for it to be better or efficient.
